Usage:
	SwfHtmlMaker [options] File/Directory

-h,     --help: Brings up the list of commands and their descriptions.
-r, -R, --recursive: Goes through all the files and folders looking for .swf and running -s on them all. Note: Must come at the end and will ignore every other command.
-v,     --verbose: Prints the action it is currently doing.
        --ignore: A list of names to ignore. Use the wildcard (*) for more general ignorance. (Case insensitive.)
